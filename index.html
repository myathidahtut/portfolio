About Me

Data storytelling, grounded in systems experience

I'm a Data Analyst & Data Scientist with 15 years of software engineering experience in Java, PHP, and MySQL. Before notebooks and models, I spent those years building the production systems that generate this kind of data in the first place, so I know what a "clean" dataset is hiding before I ever open it.

That systems background is now backed by a completed Master's in Data Science, AI & Digital Business from GISMA University of Applied Sciences in Potsdam. I work across the full analytics stack, from SQL and BI dashboards to deep learning models, turning raw data into decisions people can act on.

I like problems with a measurable answer: an accuracy delta, an error margin, a business question with a number attached. Every project below reports a real, checkable result.
